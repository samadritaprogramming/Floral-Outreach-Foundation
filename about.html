About Floral Outreach Foundation

Creating meaningful floral artistry while empowering our community through service, compassion, and purpose.

Our Story

Floral Outreach Foundation was created with one simple belief: flowers have the power to brighten lives, strengthen communities, and create lasting memories.

Through floral design and community outreach, we strive to share beauty beyond life's special moments while giving back to the Houston community.

Our Mission

Empowering communities through purposeful enterprise and dedicated action.

Our Vision

Custom floral-designed artistry that brings Houston beauty beyond the special moment.

Meet the Founder

Angeline Nguyen

Angeline Nguyen is a young florist with a love for business and giving back to the community.

As a young entrepreneur, Angeline founded Floral Outreach Foundation with the belief that every bouquet should tell a story.

Through Bouquets by Allyn, Angeline hopes to share the joy flowers have brought to her own life while using her business as a way to give back, believing that every bouquet can spread beauty, create meaningful memories, and make a positive impact beyond the flowers themselves.

Growing Together

Every arrangement represents more than flowers—it represents hope, generosity, and a commitment to serving others. Thank you for helping us continue our mission to spread kindness throughout the community.
