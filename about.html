About Floral Outreach Foundation

Every bouquet tells a story.

Our Story

Floral Outreach Foundation was created with the belief that flowers can do more than brighten a moment — they can create connections, memories, and meaningful impact.

Through purposeful floral artistry and dedication to the community, Floral Outreach Foundation works to bring beauty beyond the special moment.

Meet the Founder

Angeline Nguyen is a young florist with a love for business and giving back to the community. As a young entrepreneur, Angeline founded Floral Outreach Foundation with the belief that every bouquet should tell a story. Through Bouquets by Allyn, Angeline hopes to share the joy that flowers have brought to her own life while using her business as a way to give back, believing that every bouquet can spread beauty, create meaningful memories, and make a positive impact beyond the flowers themselves.
