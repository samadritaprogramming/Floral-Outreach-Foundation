Meet the Founder

Angeline Nguyen

Angeline Nguyen is a young florist with a love for business and giving back to the community.

As a young entrepreneur, Angeline founded Floral Outreach Foundation with the belief that every bouquet should tell a story.

Through Bouquets by Allyn, Angeline hopes to share the joy that flowers have brought to her own life while using her business as a way to give back, believing that every bouquet can spread beauty, create meaningful memories, and make a positive impact beyond the flowers themselves.
